Why VacciFast?

Entire World has come to a stand still with this ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. All necessary measures are being taken by the Government Of India in order to curb the widespread. And at present its every citizen's responsibility to follow Government guidelines get immunized at the earliest.

At present only 18% of India's population is fully immunized. In order to fasten this process here'd presnting "Mobile Vaccination Drive"

Mobile Vaccination Drive would help in targeting:

  • Smaller areas based on the pin codes & rural areas
  • Smaller the target leading to better efficiency
  • Door step vaccination, no hustle
  • Systematic approach
  • Dashboard published as per pin code , at a glance overview of the particular region


Steps to be Followed


Register Yourself and Login to the Portal.



Create your Profile.



View slots available for your pincode.



Select slot for vaccination, view the map for exact address.



After booking a slot, wait for slot to be confirmed. You would be updated via email.



On the Date of Vaccination visit the Van and can get your dose!
